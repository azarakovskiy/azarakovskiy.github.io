What else is there in Latvia and Riga?

We really would like you to also enjoy a pearl of the Baltic states: beautiful Riga since you're going there anyway. üòõ
Here some tips and recommendations:

  • Walk up on the St Peter's tower for a great view of Riga.
  • Take a walk in Old Riga (Vecriga) and check Jugend style streets and districts (known by you as art nouveau).
  • For art lovers: check National art museum and Riga Beurs.
  • If it is warm and sunny outside, consider going 30 mins away from Riga to the famous sea resort Jurmala. Looong and wide white sand beaches, pine trees forest. üèñÔ∏è Go to Mayori with a train and walk down to Jomas street towards Dzintari and the beach. Don't forget a swimsuit.
  • You fancy a majestic castle with gardens, done by the same architecture as Versailles in Paris? One or two hours car or bus ride to Rundale Palace.
  • Adventurous? Go to Tarzan obstacle adventure park in Sigulda! Just great fun! Please take care, you need your legs.
  • For nature walks: Sigulda & Turaida

Please let us know if you want to walk the most interesting and famous streets of Old Riga and hear some stories.
We might be able to walk you around on Sunday evening or Monday, or simply ask friends from Riga who willing to show you the city.
